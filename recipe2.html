Lasagna

Description:

Layers of pasta sheets, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese come together to create a comforting and indulgent Italian favorite. Baked to perfection, lasagna offers a delightful blend of flavors and textures in every bite.

Ingredients:

    • 1 pound ground beef
    • 1 onion, chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
    • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
    • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato sauce
    • 2/3 cup water
    • 2 teaspoons sugar
    • 2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 2 cups ricotta or cottage cheese
    • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
    • 2 eggs, beaten
    • 9 cooked lasagna noodles, drained

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until meat is browned; drain excess fat.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, water, sugar, basil, and salt. Simmer for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta or cottage cheese, 2 cups mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, parsley, and beaten eggs.
  4. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  5. In a 9x13-inch baking dish, layer the cooked lasagna noodles, meat sauce, and cheese mixture. Repeat the layers.
  6. Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella cheese on top.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until bubbly and golden.
  8. Let it stand for 15 minutes before serving. Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
