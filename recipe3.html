Omelette

Description:

The versatile omelette is a quick and customizable dish made by whisking eggs and cooking them with various fillings. Whether filled with cheese, vegetables, or meats, the omelette is a delicious and nutritious breakfast or brunch option.

Ingredients:

  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter or cooking oil
  • Filling of your choice (e.g., shredded cheese, diced vegetables, cooked ham)
  • Fresh herbs for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, beat the eggs and milk together. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Heat butter or oil in a non-stick skillet over medium-high heat until melted and hot.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet, swirling it around to cover the bottom evenly.
  4. As the edges set, gently lift them with a spatula, allowing the uncooked eggs to flow underneath.
  5. Once the omelette is mostly set but still slightly runny on top, add your chosen filling on one side of the omelette.
  6. Fold the other side of the omelette over the filling, creating a half-moon shape.
  7. Cook for an additional minute or until the omelette is fully set and the filling is heated through.
  8. Slide the omelette onto a plate, garnish with fresh herbs if desired, and serve immediately.
