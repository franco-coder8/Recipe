Spaghetti Bolognese

Description:

A classic Italian dish featuring a rich and savory meat sauce made with ground beef, onions, garlic, and tomatoes. The sauce is simmered to perfection and served over al dente spaghetti, creating a hearty and satisfying meal.

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 300g spaghetti
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large pan, brown the ground beef over medium heat.
  2. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and saut√© until the onion is translucent.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20-30 minutes.
  4. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  5. Serve the bolognese sauce over the cooked spaghetti, and top with grated Parmesan cheese.
